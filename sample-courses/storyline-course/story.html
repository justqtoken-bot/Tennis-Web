üéØ Active Listening Skills

The Foundation of Great Service

Active listening is more than just hearing words. It's about understanding the complete message being conveyed by your customer.

üí° Key Techniques:

  • Give full attention: Make eye contact and avoid distractions
  • Ask clarifying questions: "Can you tell me more about..."
  • Paraphrase back: "So what I'm hearing is..."
  • Show empathy: "I understand how frustrating that must be"

Remember: Customers want to feel heard and understood before you offer solutions.
